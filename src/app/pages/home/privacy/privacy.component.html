01

Introduction

Kinetic Authority ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Kinetic Authority mobile application and web platform. This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you visit or make a booking from our service.

We believe in "Kinetic Authority"—providing a high-velocity service that never compromises on the safety and integrity of your personal data. By using our service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

02

Data Collection

User Accounts

We collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, and preferred payment methods to create a seamless concierge experience.

Booking History

Destination data, travel frequency, and seat preferences are stored to curate your future journeys and optimize fleet availability.

Real-Time Geolocation

To provide accurate pick-up services and live "Trip Progress" rail updates, we collect precise location data from your device while the app is in use. You can control this in your device settings.

03

Data Usage

Route Optimization

We process historical travel patterns to deploy fleet vehicles where they are needed most, significantly reducing wait times and minimizing our environmental impact.

Service Updates

Information is used to send automated alerts regarding trip status, potential delays, and critical service changes affecting your routes.

04

Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. However, we share data with vetted partners who enable the Kinetic Authority ecosystem:

Payment Processors
Transit Partners
Analytics Providers
Legal Authorities
05

User Rights

The Right to Access

Request a comprehensive digital copy of all data we hold on your profile.

The Right to Erasure

Permanently delete your account and associated booking history from our servers.

The Right to Rectification

Update incorrect or outdated information at any time through your account portal.
